HIKING ESSETIONALS

Picking the right Hiking Gear!

The nice thing about beginning hiking is that you do not really need any special gear, you can probably get away with things you already have. Let is start with clothing. A typical mistake hiking beginners make is wearing jeans and regular clothes, which will get heavy and chafe wif they get sweaty or wet.

Understand Your Map & Timing
